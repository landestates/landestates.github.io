Real Estate Properties North America is a registered Washington State Sole Proprietorship. The principles of the organization are Karl & Carlie Erdmann.


Our mission is to develop vacant land in communities with organic growth. We accomplish this by acquiring undeveloped, developeding, and distressed properties, and locating owners ready to make use of the land, allowing counties to collect taxes and estate owners. We believe in hiring locally first and are active members of the Assets and Opprotunity Network.
